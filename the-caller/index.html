The Caller

A friendly voice, a real-sounding problem, and three chances to say the one sentence that feels rude.

Why this is hard. Social engineering works because refusing feels impolite. The safest sentence — “I’ll call you back on the number we already have” — is also the one that feels most like accusing someone of lying. This activity is practice at saying it.
